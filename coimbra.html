Discover the largest academic city in Portugal

Coimbra is a city that combines history, culture, and entertainment. It is home to the University of Coimbra, founded in 1290, one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Europe.

Coimbra

The city preserves an unparalleled historical and cultural heritage. From the top of the “Cabra”, the Tower of the university, you can admire the landscape of the city and the Mondego River.

In the Biblioteca Joanina , there are thousands of rare books, secured by bats that keep insects away. Academic life is a celebration, with traditions like ‘Queima das Fitas’ and ‘Latada’ filling the streets.

Coimbra is an unforgettable experience that blends the past and the present.  Are you ready to explore Coimbra?

Enjoy the academic life

Latada
The “Latada” is a celebration that marks the beginning of the academic year, usually held in October. This event is dedicated to the freshmen, the new students who embark on their academic journey in Coimbra.

During the Latada, the freshmen parade through the city streets, dressed in traditional costumes and carrying soda cans tied with ropes.

The noise of these cans resonates through the streets, symbolizing the students’ entry into university life. The atmosphere is contagious as the freshmen are accompanied by their mentors and veterans, strengthening bonds of camaraderie and enthusiasm.
Queima das fitas
The “Queima das Fitas” is Coimbra’s most emblematic academic event. Held at the end of the academic year, usually in May, this celebration is marked by an intense week of processions, concerts, parties, and cultural events.

The highlight of the “Queima das Fitas” is the emotional ceremony of burning cotton ribbons – used to attach to the folders where students keep their notes. Each ribbon, with distinct colors representing each faculty and course, symbolizes the successful completion of another academic year.
Benção das Pastas
The ‘Bênção das Pastas’ in Coimbra is a significant event for university students, marking the closure of an academic stage and symbolizing the transition to new challenges. The ceremony takes place at the Sé Nova de Coimbra, where students receive blessings from Bishop D. Virgílio Nascimento Antunes. It is a memorable event that touches the hearts of students, accompanied by their family and friends.
Serenata Monumental
The “Serenata Monumental” is one of the most memorable moments in Coimbra’s academic life, especially during the “Queima das Fitas.”

Held in the Pátio das Escolas of the University of Coimbra at midnight, when the clock strikes twelve, the first guitar chords resonate through the courtyard, echoing the tradition and emotion surrounding this event.

Students sing fado and other traditional songs, creating a magical atmosphere filled with nostalgia. It is a moment of deep pride and reverence, honoring not only student culture but also the rich history of the city of Coimbra.
